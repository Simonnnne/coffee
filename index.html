Sunrise Brew

A double shot of espresso swirled into pulpy orange juice over ice. Perfect for those who can't choose between coffee or juice – now you don't have to!

Avocado Latte

A smooth blend of espresso, brown sugar, whole milk, and creamy avocado, crowned with cold foam and fresh avocado cubes. Indulgent and thick with every sip—this brew is sure to avo-cuddle your day with comfort!

Island Long Black

A bold long black meets the bright, refreshing lift of coconut water. It’s a surprising balance—where deep, rich espresso collides with island lightness. Simple. Delicious. Served hot or cold.

Iced Coconut Coffee

Wake up with this breezy iced drink—a combination of coconut milk, juice, and espresso. Lightly creamy with a tropical touch, it’s an energizing way to start your day.

Oatmilk Flat White

A single shot of espresso with oatmilk. Perfect for those who prefer a gentler coffee experience—this flat white leans on the milky side, with just a hint of espresso. Topped with delicate latte art for a simple, beautiful finish.

Mango Shake

The best drink ever—fresh mango, ice, and water, blended to icy perfection. Bright, refreshing, and perfectly frosty, it’s everything you crave in a mango shake, perfected.

Manila Smog

A messy blend of banana, chocolate, and espresso over ice—its swirling layers give it a look as gritty as Manila's smog. For a sweet finish, top with whipped cream or ice cream. Bold, refreshing, and delightfully chaotic!

Sunset Brew

A warm blend of strong coffee, condensed milk, and a hint of cinnamon and spices. Smooth, aromatic, and perfect for those quiet moments.

Barley Tea

It’s barley. It’s tea. Tastes like burnt rice, but in a strangely comforting way. Just a humble, toasted brew for when you need something oddly simple.
